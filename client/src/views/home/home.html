Logan Leaves A Lot
A Chapter Book for Ages 7-10
The First in a Series

Hi. My name is Logan. As you can probably tell from this photo, I like reading. I even found time to read at the beach.

I definitely didn’t have time to read while I was on an exciting adventure. I am a boy who loves to take trips to places that I haven’t ever been. That’s where the excitement begins.

If you read this book, you will meet many interesting and fun characters. These characters need my help and I plan on not letting them down.

Wish me luck!
